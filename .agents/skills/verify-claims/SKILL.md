# Verify Claims

When making a claim about how a specific tool, library, or platform works, do not rely on general knowledge alone — domain details are often counterintuitive.

Instead: check documentation, source code, or run a diagnostic command. If you cannot verify quickly, qualify the claim: "I believe", "check whether", "as I understand it."
